Governance Stress-Tests

Yukti Essay Canon

Yukti is where the concepts are developed in public: AI infrastructure, operational capacity, digital public infrastructure, capability formation, technology as an institutional problem, and middle-power strategy.

  • The Stack Beneath the InterfaceWhy subsidized AI access does not yet build AI capability.
  • Operational Capacity Is AI CapabilityThe scarce layer is not access to models, but the institutional ability to use them well.
  • Digital Public Infrastructure and Its LimitsPublic rails can shape AI adoption; they cannot supply the compute, energy, or institutional judgment AI requires.
  • Capability Formation, Not Technology AdoptionAccess can be rented. Adoption can be subsidized. Capability has to compound.
  • Why Technology Is an Institutional ProblemEfficiency can be bought. Judgment, recourse, and legitimacy have to be built.
  • The Dynamic Trilemma of Technology StrategySovereignty, frontier access, and efficiency cannot all be maximized; what matters is what each choice leaves behind.
  • The Uneven Acceleration ProblemAI can make generation faster before institutions become better at absorbing what gets generated.
  • The AI Market Is Not One MarketBuyers enter linked markets for models, infrastructure, coordination, and institutional assurance.
